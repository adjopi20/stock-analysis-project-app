About Us

Welcome to our platform, where the world of Indonesian stocks is brought to your fingertips in a way you’ve never seen before. This website is designed to help you make informed investment decisions by providing comprehensive insights into stocks listed on the Indonesian Stock Exchange (IDX).

Our Mission

Our mission is to simplify the complex world of stock analysis by gathering, combining, and presenting data in a way that allows you to easily compare different stocks across the same sector or industry. Unlike many other platforms that focus on analyzing individual stocks in isolation, we provide a broader perspective by comparing multiple stocks side by side using key financial metrics.

We scrape real-time data from the IDX and merge it with information from Yahoo Finance to deliver a robust set of analytical tools. Our platform offers simple yet powerful analyses that let you compare stocks based on essential financial metrics like Book Value, Price-to-Earnings Ratio (PE), Earnings Per Share (EPS), Revenue, and more. To visualize these comparisons, we provide a variety of charts, including histograms, line charts, column charts, and pie charts. These tools help you understand how a stock performs not just in isolation but in relation to its peers, giving you a clearer picture of its potential.
